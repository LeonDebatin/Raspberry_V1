Controls

Scent Selection

Click on any of the colored circles on the selection page to activate the corresponding scent formula. The selected scent will be highlighted and begin diffusion according to your configured settings.

Diffusion Duration

Use the diffusion duration slider to control the overall smell strength. The slider adjusts how long each scent is active within the fixed 60-second cycle, ranging from 5 to 60 seconds in 5-second increments.
